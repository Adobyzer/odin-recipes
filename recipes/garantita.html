Description

"Algerian dish of Hispanic-Oranese origin made from chickpea flour, resembling a flan."

As an appetizer, nothing beats a small chickpea gratin. Usually, it's a meal on its own, but I promise we'll serve just a bit for the starter.

Ingredients

  • Milk (3 cups)
  • Olive oil (0.5 cup)
  • Eggs (2 px)
  • Chickpea flour (1 px)

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together, season with salt, pepper, and cumin.
  2. Let the mixture rest for at least 6 hours in a buttered mold, and blend every 2 hours.
  3. Blend again and bake in a preheated oven at 240Â°C (th.8) for 30 to 35 minutes.
